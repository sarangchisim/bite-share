Kacchi Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg mutton (cut into large pieces)
  • 2 cups fragrant basmati rice
  • 2 large onions (sliced and fried golden)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mustard oil
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 3-4 cinnamon sticks
  • 5-6 cardamom pods
  • 6-7 cloves
  • 1/2 cup milk with a pinch of saffron
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the mutton with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Let it rest for 2-3 hours.
  3. In a large pot, layer the marinated mutton at the bottom.
  4. Add a layer of half-cooked rice on top, followed by fried onions, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and saffron milk.
  5. Drizzle ghee over the top layer.
  6. Seal the pot with a lid and cook on very low heat for 1.5-2 hours.
  7. Mix gently before serving, ensuring the layers remain intact.
