Shorshe Ilish Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of hilsa fish (Ilish)
  • 2 tablespoons mustard paste
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Clean the hilsa fish pieces and marinate with turmeric powder and salt.
  2. Heat mustard oil in a pan. Add the marinated fish pieces and fry them for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  3. In the same pan, add mustard paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the water, slit green chilies, and sugar (if using). Bring to a gentle boil.
  5. Carefully place the fried fish pieces back into the pan. Cover and cook on low heat for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Once the sauce thickens and the fish is cooked, remove from heat.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
